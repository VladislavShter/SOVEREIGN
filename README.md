All this happens in the background. Quietly. Without explicit consent вҖ” or with consent hidden on page 47 of a user agreement that no one reads.

This is a sandbox. And it does not work.

Modern banking apps, government apps, and even many commercial apps know what a sandbox is and block themselves upon detecting one. They check:

   в–ё  Is the process running with the correct Android user UID

Sandboxes fail on every one of these points. A banking app in Parallel Space will either not launch at all, or launch with limited functionality, or launch вҖ” and quietly inform the bank that it is running in a compromised environment. With consequences for your account.
